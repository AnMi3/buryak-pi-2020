Atmega8A

8MHZ internal oscillator:

Low: 0x84
High: 0xD9

avrdude -p atmega8 -c USBasp -U flash:w:avr_kbd.ino_atmega8_8000000L.hex -U lfuse:w:0x84:m -U hfuse:w:0xd9:m

16MHz external crystal:

Low: 0x8E
High: 0xD9

avrdude -p atmega8 -c USBasp -U flash:w:avr_kbd.info_atmega8_16000000L.hex -U lfuse:w:0x8e:m -U hfuse:w:0xd9:m
